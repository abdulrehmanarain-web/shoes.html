About Zaroon

Zaroon Couture was founded to celebrate Pakistani textiles and artisanal craft. We partner with local artisans for handwork and use responsibly sourced fabrics to create elegant garments that feel modern yet rooted in tradition. Every collection is curated with a focus on fit, fabric, and detail — meant to be treasured.

Our values:
  • Heritage-inspired design
  • Quality craftsmanship
  • Conscious sourcing
